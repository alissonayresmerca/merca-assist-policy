Merca Assist Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how this Chrome extension (hereafter referred to as "Merca Assist") collects, uses, and discloses your data.

Information We Collect

The Merca Assist collects the following information:

  • Time Preferences: You can set your expected clock-in times (entradaTime), lunch break times (almocoTime1 & almocoTime2), and clock-out time (saidaTime) through messages sent to the Extension.

How We Use Your Information

The Extension uses the collected information solely to:

  • Remind you to clock in/out at your designated times.
  • Notify you 10 minutes before your designated clock in/out times.
  • Track your clock-in/out status to identify missed entries.
  • Calculate and warn you if you exceed the maximum allowed extra work hours.

We do not:

  • Track your browsing activity outside of the notification functionality.
  • Sell or share your data with any third-party companies.

Your Choices

You can control the information collected by the Extension by:

  • Not setting your clock in/out times, since it's optional.
  • Uninstalling the Extension.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you provide from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage system is ever completely secure.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy.
